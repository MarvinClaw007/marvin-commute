Spaghetti Bolognese

45
minutes
4
serves
Easy
difficulty
⭐ 4.8
rating

📝 Ingredients

500g spaghetti
400g beef mince
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic
400g canned tomatoes
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp dried oregano
Grated parmesan

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large pan. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft (3-4 minutes).
3
Add beef mince. Cook until browned, breaking up with a wooden spoon.
4
Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for 20 minutes.
5
Drain pasta and toss with sauce. Serve with grated parmesan.

💡 Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce. This dish tastes even better the next day - perfect for leftovers!
